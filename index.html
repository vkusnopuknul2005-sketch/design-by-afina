About Afina

Afina is at the beginning of her design journey — studying, experimenting and building her taste through real projects. She is inspired by clean typography, subtle color, and thoughtful composition.

This website is a living space for her future work. As new projects appear — posters, identities, layouts — they will carefully fill this portfolio, step by step.
